About This Site

a.k.a everything you've wanted to know, but were too afraid to ask

Hi there. So you're dying to know more, huh? Well this was a good place to come, and we're here to tell you every last detail.

I ( Matt ) struggled with idea of how to propose for a long time, and those of you who know me know that I'm not exactly an outgoing person of grand romantic gestures — no Chuck Bass that's for sure ( shameless gossip girl reference ). Nor am I a fan of the traditional. So naturally, this ruled out many of the usual acts which "proposal" brings to mind. I guess I'm just weird.

Anyway, so I thought to myself while trying to sleep one night: "How can I harness my nerdy powers for romance?", and the idea for this site came to mind. The hardest part is/was/has been trying to find time to work on it while keeping it a secret ( I'm typing this while she's on the the other side of the room right now actually ).

So I hope you enjoy it!
